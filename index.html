AIRGOAL · Privacy Policy v2.0

Effective date: 21 April 2026 · Last updated: 21 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application AirGoal — Road to the Cup ("the App", "we", "our") handles information in relation to the people who play it ("you", "the user"). The App is developed and distributed by WeAreSunnyBunny ("the Developer").

1. Summary

AirGoal is a single-player arcade game. It does not require you to create an account, does not ask for personal information, and does not upload any of your gameplay or profile data to the internet. Everything you see in-game — your name, your chosen colors, your best score — is stored only on your own device.

2. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not ask for your real name, email, phone number or address.
  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone or camera.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell, rent or share personal data with third parties, because we do not collect any.

3. Information stored locally on your device

To let you continue where you left off, the App stores the following on your device using the system's local storage (not transmitted to us):

  • The player nickname you type in the profile screen.
  • Your chosen shirt color, skin tone and hair color.
  • Your best score and which ball skins you have unlocked.
  • Your music and sound-effect preferences (on/off).

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App or clearing its storage permanently removes it.

4. Advertisements

The current public version of AirGoal does not serve real advertisements. The in-game "Watch Ad · Double Score" button is a placeholder screen used only for gameplay. If a future version integrates a real mobile ad network (for example Google AdMob), this Privacy Policy will be updated at least 7 days before that version is published, and the updated version will describe which advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) may be accessed and used for frequency capping or ad measurement, in accordance with Google Play's User Data and Families policies.

5. Permissions

AirGoal does not request runtime permissions on Android. It only uses the standard INTERNET permission that is automatically declared by the Android framework and that is required for the optional future ad integration described in Section 4. No dangerous permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, etc.) are requested or used.

6. Children's privacy

AirGoal is rated for all ages (Everyone / PEGI 3). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because no personal information is collected from anyone, no special handling of children's data is required. If you believe that a child has been asked to provide personal information through the App, please contact the Developer so we can investigate.

7. Third-party services

The production version published on Google Play is distributed through Google Play Services, which may collect diagnostic and crash data on behalf of Google according to Google's own privacy policy. AirGoal itself does not integrate any analytics, crash-reporting, social-network or tracking SDK.

8. Data security

Because AirGoal stores data only locally on your device, the security of that data is provided by your device's operating system (Android) and its sandboxing of each application.

9. Your rights

You can delete all data stored by AirGoal at any time by clearing the App's storage from your device settings, or by uninstalling the App. Because no personal data is ever transmitted, there is no server-side data for us to access, correct or delete.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the App or to applicable law. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always show the date of the current version. Material changes will additionally be announced in the App's release notes on Google Play.
